BioSolutions

Not Just a Biotech Company

BioSolutions was founded by Alexandros Kalafatelis in 2019.

We are a Greek company based at Psachna, Evia and we specialize in the sections of research and development of in vitro diagnostic products for clinical applications. Our products are accredited by ISO.


Alexandros Kalafatelis Founder, CEO

Alexandros Kalafatelis founded BioSolutions in 2019 while studying for his degree at the University of Athens (NKUA), using the research facilities of the university and from getting meaningfull advices from his college proffesors. His background can be described as diverse, because he holds a degree in Biomedical Sciences (Hons) from the University of East London and he is about to receive his second bachelors degree(BSc) in Electrical Engineering from the University of Athens. Currently, he also works as a Biologist at the Metropolitan Hospital in Athens, supervising the laboratories of Hematology and Microbiology.

Apart from his great academic achievements, he is also political active. In 2019 we was chosen as a Member of the Hellenic Student Parliament.

His goal is to merge healthcare and engineering.
